Dungeons and Dragons

Dungeons and Dragons is a tabletop roleplaying game. It's usually played with 3-5 players. The Dungeon Master creates the story, and the players roll dice and roleplay to interact with it. I've been playing Dungeons and Dragons, or "D&D," since I was fourteen years old! I usually play elves and warlocks/sorcerers. Click here to learn more about D&D!

Gardening

One of the things I'm passionate about doing in my garden is planting pollinator-friendly plants. Pollinators are often threatened species or don't have enough resources, and these plants help support them! I get my seeds through Operation Pollination, which offers free packets of pollinator-friendly wildflower seeds. Get them here!

Horror Podcasts

I started listening to podcasts when I started working behind the scenes in libraries--it can get very quiet! My favorite genre is horror, and there are some truly exciting horror podcasts out there. I recommend The Magnus Archives, its sequel The Magnus Protocol, Lore, and (if you're looking for some heartwarming with your horror) Welcome to Night Vale.
